# Finance & Financial Modeling Portfolio

This repository presents a collection of financial analysis and financial modeling projects developed to demonstrate practical application of corporate finance, valuation, and capital market concepts.

The projects simulate real-world tasks performed by corporate finance analysts, FP&A teams, equity research analysts, and investment banking professionals.

The objective of this portfolio is to apply financial theory to decision-making situations including investment appraisal, valuation, financial forecasting, and merger analysis.

---

## Project 1 — Capital Budgeting Investment Decision
A 5-year financial model evaluating whether a company should launch a new FMCG product line. The model forecasts operating cash flows, incorporates working capital requirements, and evaluates the investment using Net Present Value (NPV) and Internal Rate of Return (IRR).

**Key Concepts:**  
Capital budgeting, free cash flow, time value of money, project evaluation.

---

## Project 2 — Financial Statement Analysis (ITC Ltd)
A historical financial analysis of ITC Ltd using ratio analysis and DuPont decomposition to assess profitability, efficiency, liquidity, and leverage. The project evaluates operational performance and drivers of return on equity.

**Key Concepts:**  
Ratio analysis, ROE decomposition, working capital efficiency, financial health assessment.

---

## Project 3 — Pricing & Revenue Forecasting
A demand and pricing analysis using consumer willingness-to-pay data. The model constructs a demand curve and estimates revenue and profitability across multiple price levels to identify the optimal pricing strategy.

**Key Concepts:**  
Revenue forecasting, contribution margin, demand elasticity, decision analysis.

---

## Project 4 — 3-Statement Financial Forecast Model
A fully linked financial model integrating the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. The project evaluates how growth and working capital requirements affect liquidity and financing needs.

**Key Concepts:**  
Financial forecasting, working capital modeling, liquidity analysis, cash flow planning.

---

## Project 5 — Company Valuation (DCF) — Nestlé India
A discounted cash flow valuation estimating intrinsic firm value based on projected free cash flows and cost of capital. The analysis derives enterprise value, equity value, and an implied intrinsic share price.

**Key Concepts:**  
DCF valuation, WACC estimation, terminal value, intrinsic valuation.

---

## Project 6 — Portfolio Risk & Return Analysis
A portfolio optimization analysis using Modern Portfolio Theory. The model calculates returns, volatility, correlations, and evaluates diversification benefits across multiple equities.

**Key Concepts:**  
Risk measurement, correlation, diversification, portfolio optimization.

---

## Project 7 — M&A Accretion/Dilution Analysis
A merger model evaluating a hypothetical acquisition of Britannia Industries by ITC Ltd. The project analyzes transaction financing, pro forma earnings, and EPS accretion/dilution to assess shareholder impact.

**Key Concepts:**  
Merger modeling, transaction analysis, synergy evaluation, investment banking.
